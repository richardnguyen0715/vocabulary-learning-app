KHAC PHUC SU CO 
=============== 
 
1. UNG DUNG KHONG MO DUOC: 
   - Kiem tra Windows Defender/Antivirus 
   - Click phai vao file .exe > Properties > Unblock 
   - Chay bang quyen Administrator 
 
2. KHONG KET NOI DUOC DATABASE: 
   - Kiem tra MongoDB service: 
     net start MongoDB 
   - Neu loi, cai lai MongoDB 
 
3. KHONG TRA CUU DUOC TU DIEN: 
   - Kiem tra ket noi Internet 
   - Su dung che do "Quick Add" thay the 
 
4. UNG DUNG CHAY CHAM: 
   - Bo tick "Auto lookup English meaning" 
   - Su dung nut "Quick Add (No Lookup)"
